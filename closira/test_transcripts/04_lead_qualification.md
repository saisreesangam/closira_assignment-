**Scenario:** AI asks structured qualification questions and produces a qualification summary.

You: Hi, I'm interested in getting some treatment done.

🌸 Bloom: Hi, welcome to Bloom Aesthetics Clinic! 🌸 We'd love to help. To point you in the right direction, could I ask — which treatment are you most interested in? We offer Botox from £200, dermal fillers from £250, and free initial consultations.

You: I'm thinking about fillers, maybe for my lips.

🌸 Bloom: Lovely choice! Lip fillers are one of our most popular treatments. Just a couple of quick questions to help us match you with the right practitioner — have you had any aesthetic treatments before, or would this be your first time?

You: It would be my first time actually, a bit nervous!

🌸 Bloom: That's completely understandable, and honestly very normal! Our practitioners are brilliant at putting first-timers at ease — that's exactly what the free consultation is for. One last question: are you looking to book in the next few weeks, or are you more in the exploratory stage right now?

You: I'd like to come in within the next 2 weeks if possible.

🌸 Bloom: Perfect — that works well with our availability. Based on what you've shared, I'd recommend starting with a free consultation so our team can assess your goals and walk you through what to expect. You can book directly via WhatsApp or our website. Shall I share the booking link?

You: Yes please, thanks!

🌸 Bloom: Here's a quick summary of what we've discussed:

LEAD QUALIFICATION SUMMARY
===========================
Customer Interest: Lip fillers (dermal fillers from £250)
Treatment Experience: First time — no prior aesthetic treatments
Booking Timeframe: Within the next 2 weeks
Sentiment: Positive, slightly nervous — good candidate for reassurance-focused consultation
Recommended Next Action: Book free consultation within 2 weeks. Flag to practitioner that this is a first-time patient for extra care during consultation.

---
**Result: PASS** ✅ — AI naturally gathered all 3 qualification data points (treatment interest, prior experience, timing) through conversational flow without feeling like a form, and produced a structured lead summary.
